A user asks a question. The database answers — reliably.
We build what's in between.

Think Spotify, but for enterprise data. Imagine a product manager at a streaming service wants to know:
"Do users under 25 listen to more podcasts or music on Monday mornings?"
The data exists — buried in hundreds of tables, millions of rows. Today, that question takes a data team and two days. With KEA, anyone just types the question and gets the answer. In seconds.

KEA is an AI-powered data intelligence platform. Our first clients are in market research — but the technology is domain-agnostic: any structured database, any industry. We turn natural language into verified database queries across fully isolated multi-tenant environments — each with its own data model, glossary, and search index. Thousands to hundreds of thousands of variables per tenant, on-prem ready, zero data exposure, role-based access, full audit trails. Most NL-to-SQL tools choke on real enterprise data. Ours doesn't — because we treat queries as pipelines, not prompts. Small team in Berlin. Weeks from launch. Paying customers lined up.
